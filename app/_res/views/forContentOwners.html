For Content Owners

At heart we’re music lovers and record collectors first. DJing and this podcast grew out of that love. We just wanted to share our favorite music and maybe give a tiny bit of exposure to musicians who might otherwise be forgotten. We’re not a for profit operation and we don’t make any money from our podcast or this website. It’s our hope that if a listener hear’s something they like, they’ll buy the record, CD or some type of legitimate download. Even though our intentions are pure we realize that some content owners desire a greater degree of control. If, for whatever reason, you would like us to remove material for which you own the copyright, drop us a line and we’ll remove it and make a note not to use material from that artist in the future.
